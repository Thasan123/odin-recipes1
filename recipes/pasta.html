Pasta

Description

Simple, delicious and easy to make.

A flavorful pasta for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dente and set to the side
  2. Add olive oil to pan and heat over medium heat
  3. Add cherry tomatoes to pan and cook until tender
  4. Add pasta to pan and season with salt. Cook for 2 minutes
  5. Add basil and parmesan and stir until well coated
  6. Take pan off heat and let sit for 5 minutes
  7. Plate and season to taste as required
