Cheese Sandwich

Description

Crispy, gooey, goodness!

Ingredients

  • Bread Slices
  • Processed Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter bread slices
  2. Grill over medium heat until golden brown
  3. Add cheese
  4. Turn heat to low and cover pan with lid for 2 minutes
  5. Take off heat and plate
  6. Serve with side of tomato soup if desired
